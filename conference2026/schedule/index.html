Program at a glance

The detailed program will be published closer to the event. The conference runs over three days at DTU, Copenhagen.

Day 1 — Mon 12 Oct

Welcome, keynote, contributed talks, and an evening poster session and reception.

Day 2 — Tue 13 Oct

Keynotes, contributed talks, lightning talks, and community discussions.

Day 3 — Wed 14 Oct

Hands-on workshops and tutorials, satellite sessions, and closing remarks.

Full schedule to be announced.

Workshops & satellite sessions

As in previous years, the conference includes hands-on workshops and tutorials on the scverse ecosystem and satellite sessions hosted by partners. Topics and sign-up details will be shared with the full program.
