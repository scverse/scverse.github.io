## Getting to Copenhagen

<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>By air</h3>
    <p>Copenhagen Airport (CPH) is the main international gateway, well connected across Europe and beyond, with a direct metro and train link to the city centre.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>By train</h3>

  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Local transport</h3>

## Visas

Denmark is part of the Schengen Area. If you require a visa to travel, please plan ahead — we can provide invitation letters for registered attendees on request to [conference@scverse.org](mailto:conference@scverse.org).
