Community

Everyone who uses scverse software is part of the community! There are multiple ways to engage with its other members. For a start, join us on our Zulip chat, our Discourse forum, and keep an eye on our Twitter account for the events we organise.

Contributors

All this work makes sense only in the context of the larger community of contributors and users of scverse projects. Open-source scverse projects are open to contributions, and over 150 people have contributed to scverse packages.
